7-Day Winter Itinerary for Tokyo, Japan

Day 1: Arrival and First Impressions


- Arrive at Narita International Airport (NRT)
- Transportation: Take the Narita Express to Tokyo Station (approx. 60 mins, ¥3,000).
- Check into Hotel: Shinjuku Granbell Hotel
- Address: 2-14-5 Kabukicho, Shinjuku City, Tokyo
- Cost: Approx. ¥15,000/night
- Explore Shinjuku:
- Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden (9 AM - 4 PM, ¥500)
- Dinner at Omoide Yokocho (Memory Lane): small food alleys offering various ramen (dinner avg. ¥1,500)
- Google Maps

- Lunch in Asakusa: Local eateries for tempura (avg. ¥1,500)
- Afternoon Shopping: Nakamise Street
- Ginza District: Upscale shopping and cafes.
- Dinner in Tokyo Midtown (Starbucks Reserve, avg. ¥3,000)
- Winter Illuminations (5 PM onwards) at Tokyo Midtown
- Google Maps

Day 3: Day Trip to Mt. Fuji

- Activities:
- Snow activities (skiing/snowboarding, equipment rental approx. ¥5,000)

- Dinner: Return to Tokyo, dine in Shibuya (avg. ¥2,500)
- Google Maps

Day 4: Explore Omotesando and Roppongi
